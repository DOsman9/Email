Subject Building an AI Era Economy — Perspectives from BCG
Hi [Client Name], As AI reshapes how economies grow and compete, we wanted to share three pieces on what it takes to win in the AI era:
How States and Regions Can Accelerate Industry AI Adoption
SME adoption is the defining competitiveness challenge of the AI era. This paper outlines how governments can identify priority sectors, remove structural barriers, and move from awareness to real-world deployment at scale.
How States and Regions Can Prepare Their Workforce for the AI Transition
AI will reshape far more jobs than it eliminates — but most workforce systems weren't built to adapt at that speed or scale. This paper sets out how governments can build dynamic, employer-aligned ecosystems that keep pace with change.
How States and Regions Can Build AI Innovation Clusters That Scale
Isolated investments in startups or research rarely build durable innovation capacity. This paper sets out how governments can align institutions, capital, and strategy to build connected AI ecosystems anchored in existing industry strengths.
Behind each of these is a playbook on prioritization and roadmapping. If useful, we'd be happy to facilitate a brief discussion to explore what building an AI era economy could look like for your region. We'd love to hear from you. Best regards, [Your Name]
